I'm Tanya, a ux/ui designer at Priceline

where I do all sorts of things from user-research to design and development. Currently, I'm helping to build Priceline's internal styleguide and improve the post-booking experience. A constant internetter since 2000 and longtime history lover, I graduated with a MSIS from UT Austin in 2014 with plans to be a digital humanities librarian; however, along the way I discovered the world of product design. While these two disciplines might seem disimilar, they're really not: both require a thorough understanding of the user and involve building finely tuned tools to suit the user's needs. I especially enjoy complexity involved in product design and the insights gained by seeing your design perform (or not) in wild.
