Dwell Magazine Re-Design

Design

It's the year 2025 and Dwell Magazine has evolved! This project involved re-designing Dwell for future readers and consists of three cover designs, two front of the book layouts and one feature.

My Dwell re-design anticipates increased consumption of digital magazines by tablet users and uses the digital medium to engage users in ways traditioanl print magazines cannot. The digital Dwell magazine introduces motion to the reading experience! Instead of one gesture to nagivate through the magazine (the flipping of the page), the reader of digital Dwell is expected to navigate through the content, tapping and sliding to reveal pages and new information.

Although many Dwell covers sport a contemporary look, they often appear cluttered and busy. The re-design strives for a more refined, deliberate and balanced aeshetic. The new copy is more self-aware and playful and at times, the subject of the cover is unexpected.
