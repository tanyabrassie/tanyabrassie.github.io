I'm Tanya, and I'm a UX/UI designer.

I work at Priceline where I do all sorts of things from user research to design and development. Currently, I'm helping to build Priceline's internal styleguide and improve the post-booking experience. A constant internetter since 2000 and longtime history lover, I graduated with a Master's in Information Studies from UT Austin in 2014 with plans to be a digital humanities librarian; however, along the way I discovered the world of product design. While these two disciplines might seem totally dissimilar, they're really not as both require a thorough understanding of the user and involve building finely tuned tools to suit their needs. I especially enjoy the complexity involved in product design and the insights gained by seeing designs perform in the real world.
