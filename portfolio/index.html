Oh, uh hello!
I'm Tanya, a designer & developer.

An avid internetter since sixth grade — the year my mom finally acquiesced to my incessant pleas for dial-up (thanks Mom!) — I’m now a former designer turned software engineer (thanks Javascipt!). The past few years I’ve worked on projects big and small and in roles spanning from UX Engineer to Visual Designer. I genuinely love making things for the internet whether it’s in shapes or code, and am most happy wearing multiple hats while working on a small team with nice people.
