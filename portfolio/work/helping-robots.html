Robot Self-Help Book
Role: Design

Overview

Are you a down in the dumps robot, unsure of your place in the world? If so, this book might be for you. Created using letters from a visual alphabet, this handy manual (featured on the New York Times Best Seller list!) helps robots identify their components. A highly calibrated scoring system assigns each part type a value. Upon completion of the manual, android readers can compute their score and find their life purpose. Find it in the android self-help section of your local bookstore today!

What You'll Get in the Book:

A diagram of your parts--now with a cool transparent overlay!
A highly accurate quiz. We'll go section by section, examining your components to determine which tasks you're best suited for in life. This highly scientific method is the only way to guarantee you're on the correct path to true happiness.
