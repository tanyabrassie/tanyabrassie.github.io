Pulp Juice Bar Branding
Role: Design

Overview

Here we have a menu, coaster set and rewards card that I made for Pulp Juice Bar, a fictional juice bar I invented for the sole purpose of being able to make this set. Ever since getting my first crummy HP printer in sixth grade, I’ve really enjoyed making and printing things. Unfortunately, most of my work is digital so it’s a joy only experienced in my free time fictional projects.

For this project, I wanted Pulp to have a punchy, pop-art aesthetic and playful, self-aware attitude.
